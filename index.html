Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Australian Permanent Residency, skilled migration visas, and our services.

What is the Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)?

The Subclass 189 is a points-based permanent visa for skilled workers not sponsored by a state or employer. It allows you to live and work anywhere in Australia permanently.

How many points do I need for an Australian PR invitation?

The minimum threshold is 65 points, but most invitations are issued to applicants scoring 90 or above. Your score is based on age, qualifications, work experience, and English proficiency.

What is a skills assessment and who needs one?

A skills assessment confirms your qualifications and experience meet Australian standards. It is required for most skilled migration visas and is conducted by the relevant assessing body for your occupation.

What is a CDR and who needs to submit one?

A Competency Demonstration Report (CDR) is required by Engineers Australia to assess internationally trained engineers seeking PR in Australia. It includes Career Episodes, a Summary Statement, and CPD.
